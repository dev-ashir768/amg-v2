FAQ

Find Answers to Common Questions

What types of cargo do you transport?
We handle a comprehensive range of cargo including general freight, perishable goods, oversized equipment, hazardous materials (with proper certification), and high-value items.
Do you offer real-time tracking?
Absolutely! All shipments come with advanced GPS tracking that provides real-time location updates 24/7 through our online portal or mobile app.
What geographic areas do you serve?
AMG operates across 45+ countries with comprehensive coverage in North America, Europe, Asia, and select regions globally.
How do you ensure cargo safety?
We employ multiple security layers including GPS tracking, secure loading, trained drivers, full insurance, and 24/7 monitoring with strict compliance to all regulations.
What is your policy on late deliveries?
We maintain a 99% on-time rate. In rare delays, we proactively communicate and work expeditiously to resolve with possible compensation depending on your service agreement.
How can I get a quote?
Fill out our online form, call us, or email with shipment details. We typically provide quotes within 24 hours with competitive, transparent pricing.
