Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of cargo do you transport?
We handle a wide variety of cargo including general freight, temperature-sensitive goods, oversized equipment, hazardous materials (with proper certification), and specialized items. Our diverse fleet and experienced team can accommodate most transportation needs with proper safety measures and compliance.
Do you offer real-time tracking for shipments?
Yes! All our shipments come with advanced GPS tracking that provides real-time location updates. You can monitor your cargo's journey 24/7 through our online portal or mobile app, receiving automatic notifications at key milestones and estimated delivery times.
What geographic areas do you serve?
AMG Transport Solutions operates across North America, Europe, Asia, and select regions in other continents. We have partnerships with trusted carriers worldwide, enabling us to provide seamless international shipping services. Contact us to verify service availability for your specific route.
How do you ensure cargo safety and security?
Cargo safety is our top priority. We employ multiple security measures including GPS tracking, secure loading procedures, trained drivers with background checks, insurance coverage, climate-controlled vehicles when needed, and 24/7 monitoring. Our facilities feature advanced security systems, and we comply with all industry safety regulations.
What is your policy on late deliveries?
We maintain a 99% on-time delivery rate and take punctuality seriously. In the rare event of a delay, we proactively communicate with you, provide regular updates, and work to expedite delivery. Depending on the circumstances and your service agreement, compensation or credits may be available. We continuously analyze our performance to maintain the highest standards.
How can I get a quote for transportation services?
Getting a quote is easy! You can fill out our online quote form, call our customer service team, or email us with your shipment details including origin, destination, cargo type, dimensions, weight, and desired delivery timeframe. We typically provide quotes within 24 hours and offer competitive pricing with transparent terms.
