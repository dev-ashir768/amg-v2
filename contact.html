Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get a shipping quote?
We typically provide quotes within 24 hours of receiving your request. For urgent shipments, call us directly for immediate assistance.
What information do I need to provide for a quote?
Please provide: origin and destination, cargo dimensions and weight, type of goods, preferred shipping method, and timeline requirements.
Do you offer emergency or rush shipping?
Yes! We offer express air freight services for time-sensitive shipments. Contact our 24/7 support team for immediate assistance.
How can I track my shipment?
Use your tracking number on our website or contact our support team. We provide real-time updates throughout your shipment's journey.
